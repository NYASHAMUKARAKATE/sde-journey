Welcome

Hi, I'm Nyasha! This site is my own digital notebook where I collect stories, myths, and thoughts about giants—both the legendary kind and the personal challenges I face as a developer.

My Favourite Giants

  • Goliath – Reminds me that even the biggest problems can be solved with the right mindset.
  • Atlas – Inspires me to keep carrying my dreams, no matter how heavy they feel.
  • Ymir – Represents new beginnings and the raw creative energy I try to bring to my projects.

Did You Know?

Giants appear in stories from every continent. For me, they represent the challenges I face and the strength I find within myself to overcome them—especially when learning something new in tech!

My Reflection

As a kid, I pictured giants shaping the world. Now, I see every bug I fix and every new concept I learn as a mountain moved by my own hands.

Something Cool
